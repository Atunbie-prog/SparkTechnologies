Our Story

The SparkTech Vision

Founded in Lagos, SparkTech was born out of a simple observation: the digital divide in Nigerian education was growing. Traditional laptops were often too expensive, too fragile, or too complex for large-scale school deployments.


We saw Chromebooks as the perfect solution — durable, cloud-ready, and incredibly cost-effective. Today, we are proud to be one of Nigeria's most trusted suppliers of Google-certified hardware for schools, learning centres, and individual students.


From a single school in Lagos to deployments across all 36 states, SparkTech has grown because we put our clients first — every time.
